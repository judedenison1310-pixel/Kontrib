## Vision

To be the trusted home for every African group's money — making contributions, savings and disbursements transparent, simple and stress-free, so groups can focus on what they're building together instead of arguing over who paid.

---

## What Kontrib Does

Kontrib is a mobile-first app that lets any group — family, association, church unit, cooperative, office Ajo, alumni set, friends saving for a trip — collect contributions, track who paid, and prove every naira that goes out. No more spreadsheets, screenshots or "Please send me your receipt again."

**Who it's for:** Group admins, treasurers and members of Ajo/Osusu groups, savings clubs, dues-paying associations, project committees and event collections.

---

## Core Features

- **Groups & roles** — Create unlimited groups. Add members from your contacts. Admins manage; members contribute and see everything.
- **Flexible project types** — Target-based collections, event collections, emergency funds, monthly contributions, yearly dues/levies.
- **Payment receipts** — Members upload proof of payment. Admins confirm or reject with a tap.
- **Disbursement tracking** — Record every payout, attach a receipt, and recipients confirm receipt directly from a notification.
- **Real-time reports** — Live contribution and disbursement reports per project, shareable with the whole group.
- **Custom group landing pages** — Each group gets a clean public link (`kontrib.app/your-group`) to share with new members.
- **Accountability partners** — Invite trusted members to review payments alongside the admin.
- **Smart notifications** — In-app, push and WhatsApp alerts so nobody misses a payment or a disbursement.
- **WhatsApp-native onboarding** — Phone + OTP signup via WhatsApp. New members are in within 30 seconds.
- **Naira-ready** — Built for Nigeria. Currency, formatting and payment culture all match how groups already work.
